About Me

My name is Lina. I am a sound designer and sound engineer. I have a bachelors degree in Audio Technology at Lulea Univerity of Technology. I find interactive sound design and immersive experiences very exciting. I have experience in working with several DAWs including Pro Tools, Ableton Live and my current standard DAW: Reaper.

I am familiar with node based programming since I've worked on many Pure Data projects and some audio implementation in Unreal Engine. I also have basic knowledge in C#.
